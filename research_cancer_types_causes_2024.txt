Summary of Cancer Types, Causes, and Recent Advances (2024)

Types of Cancer Based on Tissue Origin:
1. Carcinomas (epithelial origin, 80-90% of cases)
2. Sarcomas (connective tissues)
3. Leukemias (blood-forming cells)
4. Lymphomas (lymphatic tissues)
5. Melanomas (melanocytes in skin)

Known Causes:
- Genetic mutations (oncogenes, tumor suppressors)
- Environmental carcinogens (tobacco, radiation, chemicals)
- Lifestyle factors (smoking, diet, obesity)
- Infections (HPV, hepatitis viruses)
- Chronic inflammation and hormones

Recent Discoveries (2023-2024):
- Targeting treatment-resistant prostate cancer
- New role of SIRPA gene in melanoma
- Impact of DNA unwinding from histones on cancer behavior

These findings highlight cancer heterogeneity driven by tissue origin, genetics, environment, and biology, affecting diagnosis and treatment.
