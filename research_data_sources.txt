Reliable sources for up-to-date cancer research data include:
- Cancer Research Data Commons (https://datacommons.cancer.gov/explore/datasets)
- CDC's Cancer Data and Statistics (https://www.cdc.gov/cancer/data/index.html)
- National Cancer Institute's Data Catalog (https://www.cancer.gov/about-nci/organization/cbiit/catalog)

Tools and platforms for automated research data extraction and NLP include:
- CLAMP
- cTAKES
- MetaMap
- Covidence
- John Snow Labs' Healthcare NLP

These tools are widely used for extracting and analyzing biomedical concept entities.
